Recipe of the Month

Recipe name

Ingredients

•140g self-raising flower
• Shake of salt
• 2 tbsp caster sugar
• 125m milk
• 1 large egg
• 1 tbsp Olive oil
• Maple Syrup
• Bacon

Method

First cook your crispy bacon…
1. Lay bacon on a cold oven proof tray and put it in a cold oven
2. Turn the oven on to 200’c and set the time for 20mins
3. It may be done slightly before, when it is, remove the oven and put the bacon on some kitchen roll to soak up any liquid
4. Whilst the bacon is cooking, mix together the flour, salt, sugar. If you can be bothered, sieve it, it will taste a bit better but not much
5. Lightly beat the egg, add the milk and oil and mix
6. Mix the two mixtures together. They should form a nice batter with a little beating and mixing. It will be quite a thick mixture
7. When the Bacon is done, heat a frying pan to a medium heat
8. Add some mixture, it will be thick, that’s fine
9. Lay a piece of bacon on top
10. When the batter starts to bubble, flip it over
11. When both sides are browned lightly, its ready to eat!
